Overview Of Embassy Knowledge Park

Embassy Knowledge Park sadahalli is an upcoming integrated development in North Bangalore set to bring about new dimensions in the experience of business, living and lifestyle. Set in sadahalli, which is one of the fastest-growing growth corridors of Bangalore, Embassy Knowledge Park sadahalli is poised to be a landmark project offering world-class infrastructure, superb connectivity, and sound investment opportunity.

With its unique position and futuristic master planning, the Embassy sadahalli Bangalore project is expected to turn out to be one of the most coveted spots for business, investors and home buyers in the future. The project is developed by Embassy Group, which is known for its expertise in developing high-end projects.

Embassy Knowledge Park North Bangalore, a brainchild of the famous Embassy Group, intends to develop an ecosystem consisting of architecture, sustainability, and top-notch facilities. If you are searching for any upcoming projects in Sadahalli Bangalore or even looking at upcoming projects around Sadahalli with great investment value, then this project will interest you a lot.

About Developer

Embassy Group is amongst the most successful and diversified conglomerates operating in the field of real estate in India, a group that has taken more than three decades to shape the corporate, residential and hospitality sectors of Bangalore and the surrounding region. Having been founded in 1993 by Jitu Virwani, the Embassy Group has metamorphosed from an exclusive Bangalore based development firm into a pan-Indian and international real estate entity whose range encompasses office parks, luxury residences, retail centres, industrial warehouses, hospitality and educational facilities. With Embassy Knowledge Park at Bettahalsur, North Bangalore, the Group combines all these strengths in one of the most strategically located residential micro-markets in the city.
